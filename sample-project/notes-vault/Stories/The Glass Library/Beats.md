# The Glass Library — Story Beats

1. Mira arrives and is shown to her quarters by Bell. She notices that Bell's shadow falls wrong.
2. First solo shift in the lower stacks — Mira feels a text respond to her that the catalog marks as inert.
3. Bell finds her with the text and does not report it. Mira begins to trust Bell slightly more than she should.
4. Mira discovers the unlisted door. The inscription reads a fragment she recognizes from the responsive text.
5. A Compact auditor arrives for a routine inspection; Bell's behavior changes in small, calibrated ways.
6. Mira reads the full inscription on the door. It reads back.
7. Bell tells Mira what it has been waiting for. This is not a comfort.
8. Mira must decide whether to open the door or to close the book she has become a character in.
