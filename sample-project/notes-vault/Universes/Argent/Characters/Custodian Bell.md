# Custodian Bell

Bell is a clockwork construct of indeterminate age — old enough that no one in the Compact knows who built it, or precisely what its instructions were beyond "guard the stacks." It stands roughly man-height, assembled from brass and dark iron, with a face that was once expressive but has been worn smooth by centuries of use into something almost serene. Its heart is a key-wound mechanism that runs perpetually without anyone ever having wound it in living memory. Bell speaks in careful, measured sentences and never lies outright; it simply does not always answer the question that was asked.
