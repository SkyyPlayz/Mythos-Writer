# Mira Halloway

Mira is a young woman of twenty-two with perpetually ink-stained fingers, a habit of reading by touch before reading by sight, and a scholarship from the Ink Compact that she suspects was not entirely merit-based. She arrived at the Glass Library without knowing what she was being tested for, and she has spent three weeks trying to look like someone who belongs here. Her gift — a sensitivity to glass-script that goes deeper than training — makes Bell watch her with something she cannot quite read, which is an unusual problem for a librarian.
