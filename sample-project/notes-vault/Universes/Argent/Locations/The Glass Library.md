# The Glass Library

The Glass Library is a vast repository in the world of Argent where books are not printed on paper but cast in thin panels of pale glass and read by touch. It occupies a building of cold grey stone somewhere between a cathedral and an archive, with stacks that extend both upward into clerestory light and downward into levels that the official catalog does not fully account for. The Ink Compact administers it in theory; Custodian Bell administers it in practice. Knowledge here is tactile — you do not read a book so much as listen to it through your fingertips — and the deepest texts require a reader steady enough not to flinch at what they find.
