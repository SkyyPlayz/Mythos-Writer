# The Ink Compact

The Ink Compact is the governing body that oversees the production, distribution, and safekeeping of glass-script knowledge in Argent. Founded after the Dissolution — a period when unregulated reading caused several catastrophic misreadings that reshaped portions of the northern coast — the Compact licenses apprentices, audits library holdings, and maintains the sealed index of texts deemed too complete for general access. Its bureaucracy is slow and its politics are opaque, but its scholarship is genuine. The Compact does not forbid dangerous knowledge; it merely insists on controlling who is ready to hold it.
