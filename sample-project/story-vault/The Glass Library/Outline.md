# The Glass Library — Outline

## Act One: Apprenticeship
Mira Halloway arrives at the Glass Library as a new apprentice, assigned by the Ink Compact to catalog the lower stacks. She meets Custodian Bell and begins learning to read glass-script by touch. She discovers that certain volumes respond to her in ways they should not — volumes locked under the Compact's seal.

## Act Two: The Locked Door
Mira finds a door in the restricted stacks that no catalog entry describes. Her attempts to read the inscription on its glass panel draw Bell's quiet warnings and the attention of the Compact's auditors. She begins to suspect the library holds a manuscript that was never meant to be finished.

## Act Three: The Compact's Secret
Mira learns that the Glass Library itself is the manuscript — every shelf a sentence, every room a chapter — and that she is not the first apprentice sent to read it. Bell has been waiting for a reader who could reach the final page without being rewritten by it.
