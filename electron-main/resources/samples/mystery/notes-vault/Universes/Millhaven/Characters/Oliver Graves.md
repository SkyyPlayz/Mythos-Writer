# Oliver Graves (deceased)

Retired solicitor, seventy-three, Millhaven resident for forty years. Club member since 1997. He was precise, principled, and had the particular combination of integrity and stubbornness that made him a good solicitor and an occasionally difficult friend. He was estranged from his daughter Sonia for fifteen years following his divorce and had recently begun to repair that relationship. In the last weeks of his life, he discovered evidence that his professional credentials had been used to commit fraud thirty years earlier and chose, characteristically, to deal with it directly and document everything in case things went wrong. They went wrong.

**Role in story:** The victim; his thoroughness and care — the timeline document — are what ultimately bring the truth to light.
