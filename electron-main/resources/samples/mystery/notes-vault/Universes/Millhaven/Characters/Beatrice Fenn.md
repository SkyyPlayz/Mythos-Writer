# Beatrice Fenn

Founded the Wednesday Club in 1993, three years after arriving in Millhaven from London. She is sixty-nine, sharp, controlled, with the kind of self-possession that comes from having decided, at some point, that she would not be afraid of being found out because she would never be found out. She is genuinely a good person in most of the dimensions that matter — she built the club, she maintains it, she has been a genuine friend to most of its members — which makes what she and Frederick Tam did in 1995 both more and less comprehensible. She helped Frederick because he asked her to and because she trusted him, and she has spent thirty years not thinking about it.

**Role in story:** One of the two people responsible for Oliver's death; her careful management of the information she shares with Helena is itself a piece of evidence.
