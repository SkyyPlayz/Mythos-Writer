# Sonia Graves

Oliver's daughter, forty-one, who has lived in Edinburgh for fifteen years and had not visited Millhaven since her parents' divorce. She came back in November at Oliver's request, when he told her what he'd found. She is practical, self-contained, and carrying both grief and something she's still working out — the complicated feeling of having been called back by a father she'd been estranged from and arriving to find him dead. She went to Helena rather than the police because Oliver had described Helena as someone who would look at things properly.

**Role in story:** Witness and collaborator; provides the crucial information about Oliver's discovery and his plan; her presence gives the resolution its emotional weight.
