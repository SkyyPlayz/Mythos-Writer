# Timeline — The Last Year of Oliver Graves

## Spring 2025

**March** — Oliver begins sorting through his old solicitor files. He has been retired for six years but kept comprehensive records; he is clearing space.

**May** — Oliver discovers an unfamiliar document in a 1995 file: a discretionary trust in his name with a co-trustee he doesn't recognise. He sets it aside, unsure whether it was a legitimate filing he's forgotten.

## Summer 2025

**July** — Oliver brings the trust document to a former colleague in Exeter for informal verification. The colleague confirms the document is genuine but cannot find matching records in Law Society files. The filing was irregular.

**August** — Oliver begins cross-referencing with the estate records of Peter Callaway (died 1994). Finds references to estate-management services provided by Frederick Tam in the period before Callaway's family concluded probate.

**September** — Oliver contacts Beatrice Fenn and asks to borrow Wednesday Club minutes from 1995-1996. Says he is writing a memoir. Beatrice lends him three folders.

## Autumn 2025

**October** — Oliver rings Sonia in Edinburgh and tells her what he has found. Sonia agrees to return to Millhaven.

**3 November** — Sonia arrives in Millhaven and takes a room at the Anchor. She and Oliver meet twice a week. He shares what he's reconstructed of the fraud.

**Mid-November** — Oliver finishes the three-page timeline document, dating and citing every element. He places it inside *The Comfort of Strangers*, the book scheduled for the December club discussion.

**27 November** — Oliver texts Helena confirming attendance at the December club meeting. He seems to be looking forward to it.

## December 2025

**Wednesday 10 December** — Oliver telephones Frederick Tam in the afternoon and asks him to come over that evening. Oliver has the full documentation. He has also, earlier in the day, rung Beatrice.

**Wednesday evening** — Oliver does not attend the book club meeting. Helena finds him at 8pm. The second wine glass has already been washed.

**Thursday 11 December** — Police begin investigation. Toxicology pending.

**Saturday 13 December** — Sonia Graves arrives at Helena Marsh's house. The timeline document is found in *The Comfort of Strangers* during a second search of the flat.
