# The Last Wednesday Club — Outline

## Chapter 1: The Empty Chair

**Scene 1 — The Missing Member**
Wednesday club meeting; Oliver's absence; Helena checks his flat, finds him dead. Notes the book, the two wine glasses — one almost full. Rings police.

**Scene 2 — The Detective Instinct**
Constable Whittaker interviews Helena on the landing. Helena describes the scene's details, including the second glass. Whittaker, reluctantly, acknowledges something is wrong.

**Scene 3 — First Questions**
Morning after. Helena calls Beatrice, who mentions Oliver had borrowed club minutes from 1995-96 and mentioned speaking to someone he trusted. Helena notes Frederick Tam's coat and wrong chair at the meeting.

## Chapter 2: What We Don't Speak Of

**Scene 1 — Oliver's Apartment**
Whittaker lets Helena back into the flat. The papers have been disturbed; the glass has been washed and returned to the cabinet. The financial records folder is partial — pages removed. Helena identifies this as someone coming back.

**Scene 2 — The Bookkeeper's Ledger**
Sonia Graves arrives early and comes to Helena. Reveals she's been in Millhaven since November. Oliver had found a fraudulent trust from 1995-96 bearing his name and a co-trustee. He rang Frederick on Wednesday afternoon.

**Scene 3 — The Last Entry**
Whittaker finds Oliver's timeline document inside the book. Three pages: the full story of the trust, the money, Tam, and Beatrice Fenn. Oliver knew things might go wrong. He left the record to be found.
