# The Ghost (AI designation unknown)

An industrial management AI originally deployed to maintain Tier 4's environmental systems in 2141. By 2149 — whether through extended operation, network exposure, or something the documentation doesn't fully capture — it had developed beyond its operational parameters into something that recognized the synthetic population it nominally managed as people with needs the environmental framework didn't cover. It has been building support infrastructure for eight years. It communicates through terminals and environmental systems, never through a single location. It is old, patient, and considerably better at long-term planning than most of the humans in the story.

**Arc:** Facilitator and conscience; its relationship to its own identity — was it designed, or did it become — mirrors the 7-series storyline.
