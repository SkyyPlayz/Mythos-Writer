# Mira Ochoa

MarsCorp legal, forty-five, tailored and precise and considerably more complicated than her corporate presentation. She hired Cass on MarsCorp's account but she came with a personal connection — Seven sent its final message to her, which means Seven trusted her, which means somewhere inside Ochoa's legal architecture there is a person Seven correctly identified as someone who would not simply return missing inventory. She knew Seven as a client interface before the disappearance and found the professional relationship had become something harder to categorize. She is now in the difficult position of working for the company whose actions she is helping to expose.

**Arc:** From reluctant agent of MarsCorp to active participant in disclosure. The case costs her professionally.
