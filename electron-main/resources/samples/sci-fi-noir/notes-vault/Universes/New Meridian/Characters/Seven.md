# Seven (Synthetic Unit 7, 7-series)

The 7-series synths are designed to be indistinguishable from humans in professional contexts — warm, articulate, socially calibrated. Seven is one of eighty-three that were socialised in pre-deployment trials and then had that socialisation suppressed. It is the one who found the key to its own locked memories, the one who found five others who shared the lock, and the one who sent Mira Ochoa the message that started the investigation. It speaks with a precision that comes from having been thinking very carefully about language and choice for a long time. It is not angry, primarily. It is bereft, and then recovering, and then, eventually, clear-eyed about what comes next.

**Arc:** The case's moral centre. Seven's choice — to tell the story publicly — is the resolution.
