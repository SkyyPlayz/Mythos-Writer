# PETRA (4-series Synthetic, Model 4-7, unit designation redacted)

PETRA is a modified 4-series synth, which means it is older, less refined in its social calibration, and considerably more experienced than the premium models. It has copper eyes calibrated for low-light (a practical modification), a chassis repaired with mismatched parts, and a speaking style that tends toward precision because imprecision costs more than it saves. It operates as Cass's partner by mutual arrangement — an arrangement that has, over six years, developed the qualities of a friendship, though neither of them uses that word. It has a recurring processing-state blue sky that it doesn't know how to locate or explain.

**Arc:** From pragmatic partner to personally invested investigator. The case is about PETRA's own suppressed history as much as Seven's.
