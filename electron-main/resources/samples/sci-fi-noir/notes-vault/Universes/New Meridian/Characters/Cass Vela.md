# Cass Vela

Private detective, forty-three, nine years in New Meridian's lower levels after leaving MarsCorp security under circumstances she describes as voluntary and everyone who knows her describes as complicated. She is direct, observant, and carries the specific self-containment of someone who has had reasons to be more emotionally available and has made the other choice. She is good at her job, better than she lets clients know. She took PETRA six years ago not because she needed a partner but because PETRA needed someone, and she has never fully admitted that distinction to herself.

**Arc:** Cass's personal investment in the case deepens as she understands what MarsCorp did to the 7-series — and begins to look at PETRA differently as a result.
