# Director Solan

Head of MarsCorp's synthetic division for eleven years. Sixty-one, thin, with the focused management style of someone who has converted every problem in his career into a logistics problem with a budget line. He ordered the internal investigation shut not because he didn't care that the synths were missing but because he suspected, correctly, that finding them would create a disclosure problem larger than the inventory value. He is not the original architect of the memory suppression — that decision was made before his tenure — but he has maintained and defended it. He is, in the way of many people who enable institutional harm, a man who is genuinely good at other things.

**Arc:** Antagonist by position rather than psychology; his leverage is limited once the Ghost Network is involved.
