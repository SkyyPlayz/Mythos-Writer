# Story Beats — Neon Rust

## Act 1: The Investigation Begins
- Ochoa hires Cass; the case looks like a standard recovery operation
- PETRA and Cass establish that the disappearance was chosen, not accidental
- The Ghost is identified as the organizer; Tier 4 as the location

## Act 2: What's Actually Happening
- The Tier 4 facility: seven synths in recovery, processing suppressed memories
- PETRA's confession: it has its own suppressed history it doesn't know how to access
- The documentation exists; the question is leverage and timing

## Act 3: The Decision
- Cass confronts Solan: ten days and documentation
- Seven recovers and states the group's demands
- Ochoa presents the settlement option
- Seven chooses the story

## Key Structural Note
The case's emotional stakes grow from Cass's professional investment to personal, as PETRA's connection to the central mystery becomes clear. By Act 3, Cass is not pursuing this case for the client — she's pursuing it because it's the right thing to do, which is the noir detective's traditional late-story moral clarification.
