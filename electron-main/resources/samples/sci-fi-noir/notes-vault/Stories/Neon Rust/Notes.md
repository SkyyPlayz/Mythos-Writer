# Working Notes — Neon Rust

## Tone
Rain-soaked, morally textured, precise. Noir's native mode is disillusionment that doesn't curdle into cynicism — the detective sees how things are and keeps showing up anyway. Cass should be tired without being defeated.

## The Rain
New Meridian's scheduled rain is the story's recurring motif. It's beautiful, it's fake, and it works anyway — which is the story's thematic core. At the end, Cass standing in the rain is peace, not irony.

## PETRA's Communication Style
PETRA speaks with high precision because imprecision costs more than it saves — a statement about both engineering and experience. Its pauses are thoughtful, not slow. When it says something without hedging, it means it.

## New Meridian Geography
- **Tier 1-2**: Corporate/residential upper levels; good light, managed environment
- **Tier 3**: Middle residential; the mix
- **Tier 4**: Lower infrastructure; the Collective's territory; where the Ghost operates
- The dome membrane is always visible from the upper tiers; invisible from below
