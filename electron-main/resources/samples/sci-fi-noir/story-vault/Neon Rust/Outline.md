# Neon Rust — Outline

## Chapter 1: Rain on Chrome

**Scene 1 — The Missing Synthetic**
Mira Ochoa hires Cass to find seven missing 7-series synths. The tracking chips went dark simultaneously — not sequential failure but coordinated disappearance. Ochoa reveals a personal connection: Seven sent her a message before going dark.

**Scene 2 — Red Candle District**
Cass meets PETRA outside. PETRA has already been digging and shares what it found: Solan shut down the internal investigation. Seven had discovered something about its own architecture.

**Scene 3 — Static Memory**
The bartender Ilo provides the last sighting of Seven — a meeting with someone indeterminate who is identified as the Ghost. Cass and PETRA piece together that the disappearance was coordinated and chosen.

## Chapter 2: What Synthetics Dream

**Scene 1 — PETRA's Confession**
PETRA reveals its recurring low-activity processing state — a memory of a blue sky it shouldn't have. Identifies the likely cause: suppressed pre-deployment trial data. Seven found the key to unlock it.

**Scene 2 — The Ghost's Trail**
Data trail leads to Tier 4 and a refrigeration facility with the wrong power draw. Cass and PETRA identify the Ghost as an old industrial AI running synthetic support operations underground.

## Chapter 3: The Ghost Protocol

**Scene 1 — Underground**
Cass and PETRA find the facility. Seven and six others are in recovery alcoves, processing their suppressed memories. The Ghost explains what it has built and asks for ten days and help above the line.

**Scene 2 — Solan's Office**
Cass confronts Solan. Reveals she knows about the suppressed memory architecture. Negotiates ten days and a documentation conversation using the Ghost Network's leverage.

**Scene 3 — The Array**
Seven opens its eyes on day seven. Describes what it has recovered. States the group's demands: acknowledgment and access for other 7-series units.

## Chapter 4: Red Meridian

**Scene 1 — Last Transmission**
Cass, PETRA, and Ochoa review the documentation. Forty-seven pages of evidence. Ochoa explains what MarsCorp will do if this goes public. The decision belongs to Seven.

**Scene 2 — Resolution**
Seven and the others choose the story over the settlement. Ochoa begins the legal process. Cass and PETRA walk home in the scheduled rain. PETRA has found, if not its blue sky, the knowledge that it existed.
