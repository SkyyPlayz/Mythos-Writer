# Sister Marigold

The village healer has been in Ashford for thirty years, trained in practical herbalism and a variety of what she calls common-sense remedies. She is fifty-six, capable, and carries a mistake she made twelve years ago with the quiet weight of someone who has decided that carrying it is the best they can do. She is genuinely good at her work and genuinely fond of the village. She is also the person responsible for the compulsion on Fenwick's memory, which she installed with the best of intentions and has maintained with increasing discomfort. She is not the villain. She is someone who made a choice for someone else and never found the right moment to undo it.

**Role in story:** The unexpected answer at the centre of the mystery; her arc is about confession and repair.
