# Thessaly Briarbend

The village bookkeeper is forty-two, careful, observant, and one of the few people in Ashford who knows about the magical anomaly in Fenwick's accounts because she noticed a financial one first. She has a small, undeveloped talent for resonance — she can sense when something is off without knowing why, which makes her an excellent accountant and a slightly anxious person to be. She came to Rowan Ashby's shop once, years ago, to ask about the feeling she sometimes gets near certain objects, and she has been sitting with the answer ever since without acting on it.

**Role in story:** Provides the financial thread of the Fenwick mystery; potential future student.
