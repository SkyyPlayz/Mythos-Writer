# Rowan Ashby (deceased)

Wren's grandmother ran the Hearthstone shop for forty-one years and was, by all accounts, the kind of person who made the village better simply by being in it. She was precise, unsentimental, deeply warm beneath a surface she kept deliberately brisk — she believed in letting people find their own answers rather than giving them away too readily. She died at eighty-three with several problems still open (the Fenwick case among them), which the hearth implies she left on purpose: she saw more potential in Wren than she ever said aloud, and she believed the best way to teach someone to swim is to put them in the water.

**Role in story:** Absent presence throughout; her notes and hearth-memories guide Wren through the central mystery.
