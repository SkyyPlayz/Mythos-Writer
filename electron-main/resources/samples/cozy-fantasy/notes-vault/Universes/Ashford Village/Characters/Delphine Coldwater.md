# Delphine Coldwater

Delphine runs Ashford's only bakery, which she inherited from her mother and has expanded into something genuinely excellent. She does not believe in magic — or rather, she believes in *results*, and she has always gotten results through skill, hard work, and refusing to accept failure as an option. She is forty-four, tall, exacting, and the kind of woman who gives criticism as a form of respect and compliments as a form of apology. Her relationship with Wren is prickly and evolving: she came to the shop in need and left with a grudging acknowledgment that some things work even if she doesn't understand why.

**Role in story:** Represents the village's skeptical voice; her arc tracks the community's gradual trust in Wren.
