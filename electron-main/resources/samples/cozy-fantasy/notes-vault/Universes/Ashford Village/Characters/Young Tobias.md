# Young Tobias

Tobias is eleven, an orphan being raised by his uncle (the butcher), and the only person in Ashford who walked into the tea shop on the first day and asked, without preamble, if Wren would teach him. He has a demonstrable talent for the domestic magic — things stay warm near him, bread rises better when he's in the kitchen — and an earnest, slightly alarming confidence that he is going to be very good at this. Wren is teaching him basic hearth-reading on Thursday afternoons, which he takes as seriously as a surgical procedure. He is a genuinely good kid in the way that is sometimes the most disarming thing a child can be.

**Role in story:** Student; provides warmth and lightness; his arc is the next generation of the practice.
