# Sable Nightshade

The traveling merchant passes through Ashford every two to three months and has done so for twenty years. They are ageless in the specific way of people who have lived outdoors for decades and used sunscreen conscientiously, somewhere between forty and sixty, with dark eyes that catalogue everything and a voice that suggests they've said most things before. They deal in specialty goods: rare herbs, unusual papers, objects with provenance worth verifying. They have their own complicated relationship with the Compact — cooperative but not subservient — and they came to Wren's grandmother as an ally, which they now extend, cautiously, to Wren.

**Role in story:** Unlikely ally; provides the watch and the crucial piece of the mystery.
