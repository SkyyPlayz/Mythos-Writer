# Working Notes — The Hearthstone Witch

## Voice
The prose should feel warm but precise — cozy does not mean vague. Specificity is warmth. Know exactly what the tea smells like, exactly what the chalk-board says, exactly how many times Pip rotates. The magic should feel earned, not magical.

## Pip's Communication System
Pip has a consistent language:
- **Spout direction**: indicates attention or concern (pointing at someone = important)
- **Lid position**: raised slightly = proceed with caution; lid-drop = agreement
- **Heat**: warmer = trust; cooler = suspicion
- **Kettle-sounds**: low simmer = contentment; beginning-to-boil = alertness; full whistle = urgency (rare)

## The Village as Character
Ashford should feel like a real community with history. Characters should mention each other, have pre-existing relationships, surprise Wren with connections she didn't expect. The village knows things Wren doesn't, and it will tell her, in time.
